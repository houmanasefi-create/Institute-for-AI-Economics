This research underscores the potential for small nuclear power to stabilize electricity supply amidst the surging energy requirements driven by AI data center expansion.   2. Economic implications of nuclear energy versus renewable sources: There is contention over whether investing in small nuclear is more beneficial than focusing on expanding renewable energy sources.
  3. The role of government in facilitating nuclear energy adoption: Disagreement exists on how much regulatory support or intervention is necessary to promote small nuclear technologies.
