About the Lab

A modern think tank for an AI-shaped economy.

Traditional think tanks move too slowly for AI. The Institute for AI Economics is designed to publish faster, synthesize better, and focus on the real bottlenecks of power: infrastructure, incentives, and institutional adaptation.

The goal is not abstract commentary. The goal is to produce decision-grade insight for founders, operators, policymakers, investors, and institutions navigating the next economic regime.
